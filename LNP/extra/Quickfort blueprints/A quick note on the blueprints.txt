Here you go:  all the blueprints that the DF community has produced and shared.  

The organisation is lacking, since I've just left them as they come - the names have no particular order, and it can be hard to find what you're after.  Sorry about that.  

On the other hand, you can probably find in this mess something quite like what you're after - or a least something to tweak or mash up with another plan.  Some parts have a readme file, or even images - but not all.

If you come up with something cool, please share it!  The whole community loves this stuff, and it's people sharing that created this whole collection.  

Enjoy!
